Seminar: Advanced Topics on Security and Privacy for Visual and Language Generative Models

New capabilities of language and diffusion models enable applications that interact with users across different modalities, perform independent actions, and leverage external tools. In the seminar, we will study how these capabilities create new privacy and security challenges by analyzing recent papers in ML and S&P communities and connecting discovered problems to fundamental issues from previous decades. As part of the course there will be an opportunity to conduct a research project that goes deeper into these problems.

Topics

  • Week 1. Privacy. Membership Inference
  • Week 2. Privacy. Model Stealing
  • Week 3. Privacy. Differential Privacy
  • Week 4. Privacy. DP In-Context Learning
  • Week 5. Privacy. Synthetic Data Generation
  • Week 6. Privacy. Inference Privacy
  • Week 7. Privacy. Contextual Integrity
  • Week 8. Security. Jailbreaking
  • Week 9. Security. Agents and Tool Use Attacks
  • Week 10. Security. Visual Language Model Attacks
  • Week 11. Security. Backdoor Attacks
  • Week 12. Security. Denial of Service Attacks
  • Week 13. Security. System Design Discussions
  • Week 14. Presentations
